# Quick Start Guide

Get your Brand Kit Dashboard up and running in 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Create Icons (2 minutes)

1. Use an image editor or online icon generator
2. Create three PNG files:
   - `icon16.png` (16x16 pixels)
   - `icon48.png` (48x48 pixels)
   - `icon128.png` (128x128 pixels)
3. Use your brand colors and design
4. Place all three files in the `icons/` folder

## Step 2: Install Extension (1 minute)

### Chrome:
- Go to `chrome://extensions/`
- Enable "Developer mode"
- Click "Load unpacked"
- Select this folder

### Firefox:
- Go to `about:debugging`
- Click "This Firefox" ‚Üí "Load Temporary Add-on"
- Select `manifest.firefox.json`

## Step 3: Configure Brand Colors (2 minutes)

1. Open a new tab (you'll see the dashboard!)
2. Click the settings icon (‚öôÔ∏è) in the top right
3. Set your brand colors:
   - Primary Color: Your main brand color
   - Secondary Color: Your secondary brand color
   - Accent Color: For highlights and reminders
4. Enter your team name
5. Click "Save Settings"

## Step 4: Add Your Content

- **Daily Focus**: Type your main goal for the day
- **Quick Links**: Add your frequently visited sites (press **L** to quick-add)
- **Reminders**: Set important deadlines (press **R** to quick-add)
- **Notes**: Keep important info visible (press **N** to quick-add)

## Pro Tips

- **Ctrl/Cmd + K**: Quick access to settings
- **Export Settings**: Share your config with your team
- **Background**: Add a custom image URL for your brand
